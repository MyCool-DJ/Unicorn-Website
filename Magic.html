What is a Unicorn?

In business, a unicorn is a privately held startup company valued at over $1 billion. The term was coined in 2013 by venture capitalist Aileen Lee, choosing the mythical animal to represent the statistical rarity of such successful ventures. The first unicorn was Google; there is now over 600 unicorns worldwide.
